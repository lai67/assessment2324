Close to the Bustle, Yet Far from the Noise

Overveen, a serene haven nestled amidst nature, brings you the perfect blend of tranquility and accessibility. The village is a sanctuary of peace and calm, where the soft rustling of leaves harmonizes with the chirping of birds. However, its proximity to major urban hubs, such as Haarlem and Amsterdam, ensures you are never far from the vibrant pulse of city life.

Overveen caters to both work and play with effortless ease. For your daily commute or an impromptu weekend adventure, you're well-connected. A bus station, with Bus Number 81 to Haarlem at your service, offers convenience. Additionally, a train station links you directly to the heart of Amsterdam, opening the doors to an array of cultural delights, shopping districts, and job opportunities.

Here, you can embrace the serene mornings and the bustling afternoons. Overveen is not just a village; it's an exquisite balance between calm and connectivity, where you can live life at your own pace. Experience the best of both worlds, where tranquility and the city's allure coexist harmoniously.
